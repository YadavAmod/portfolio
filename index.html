About Me

As a passionate and driven Cyber Security Intern, I am dedicated to safeguarding digital assets and contributing to a safer online world. I have completed my graduation in B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, which has provided me with a strong foundation in computer systems, programming, and network technologies.

My journey in cybersecurity began with a deep curiosity for how technology works and a commitment to understanding the threats facing modern organizations. This interest has led me to pursue hands-on experience through internships, academic projects, and personal initiatives.

I have gained practical experience with key cybersecurity tools and concepts, including intrusion detection systems, vulnerability assessments, and security information and event management (SIEM) platforms. My technical skills span across network security, ethical hacking, incident response, and scripting with Python. I have also participated in Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges and contributed to open-source security projects, which have strengthened my problem-solving abilities and teamwork skills.

My portfolio highlights a variety of projects, such as conducting security audits, analyzing network traffic with Wireshark, and automating security tasks using Python. I am continuously expanding my knowledge by working towards industry-recognized certifications and staying engaged with the latest trends and threats in cybersecurity.

What motivates me most is the challenge of solving complex security issues and the opportunity to make a tangible impact in protecting digital infrastructure. I am eager to collaborate with professionals in the field, learn from real-world scenarios, and contribute my skills to innovative security solutions.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting and exploring opportunities to grow in the dynamic world of cybersecurity.
